# Tutorial: Getting Started with Answer King Ecosystem

Welcome to the **Answer King Ecosystem**, powered by the **SaaSSkul Framework**. This guide will help you set up your local environment and start using the tool effectively.

## 1. Activation
- Navigate to your WordPress Plugins page.
- Activate **Answer King Core**.
- You will see a new **SaaSSkul** menu item in your sidebar.

## 2. Setting Up Your License
- Go to **SaaSSkul > Licensing**.
- Enter your **SaaSSkul License Key**.
- If you are **Fazal Shahid Latif**, your access is automatically verified via your email: `info@saasskul.com`.

## 3. Keyword Research
- Click on **Answer King** in the sidebar.
- Enter a seed keyword (e.g., "SaaS marketing").
- View the grouped results in the Questions, Prepositions, and Comparisons tabs.

## 4. Documentation & Compliance
- Legal pages (Privacy, Terms, Cookies) are automatically generated in your **Pages** section.
- Customize them in the WordPress Editor.
